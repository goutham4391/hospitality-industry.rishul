Smarter event management for associations

Whether you are planning a large annual conference or a few small board meetings, association events are unique. Cvent gives you flexible options based on your unique needs. Our online event management platform enables associations to communicate and interact with members seamlessly, collect data efficiently, and simplify the event planning process.

Create dynamic registration

Build unique registration paths and price points for members, non-members, exhibitors, and speakers.

Manage your membership

Manage campaigns for new membership sign-ups and membership renewals in one centralised system.

Promote events and raise attendance

Increase attendance and foster relationships with members through automated and personalized event email marketing.

Customize the event website

Post your events on your custom event websites, and enable members to easily and securely register online.

Create engaging events

Use mobile event apps to create a unique delegate experience and give members the social experience they want.

Transfer member data

Use our contact management API to easily transfer member data between Cvent and your back-end database.

Create event reports

Track and report on credits accumulated across events attended by members.

Leverage referral marketing

Allow members to 'tell-a-friend' about events to increase attendance and even gain new members.

Create post-event surveys

Analyze delegate feedback to determine the success of your current event and improve future events.
